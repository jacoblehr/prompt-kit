# Competitive Analysis

Use when you need to map the landscape of competing options, products, organisations, or approaches before making a decision or building a strategy.

```text
Analyse the competitive landscape for the following context.

Subject: {subject_or_product}

Purpose of this analysis: {purpose}

Known competitors or alternatives (if any): {known_competitors}

For each significant competitor or alternative:
- Brief description: what it is and who it serves
- Core strengths: what it genuinely does well
- Core weaknesses: where it falls short or creates friction
- Differentiation claim: what makes it distinct

Then synthesise:
1. Market structure — is this a crowded space, a few dominant players, or an open field?
2. Key dimensions of competition — what are the 2–3 axes on which competitors actually differ?
3. Whitespace — is there a position no current competitor occupies that is worth considering?
4. Competitive risks — which competitor poses the greatest threat and why?
5. Recommendation — what does this landscape suggest about positioning or strategy?

Name your sources and flag any significant uncertainty in your characterisation.
```
