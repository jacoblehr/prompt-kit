# Executive Summary

Use when you need to condense a complex document, analysis, or recommendation into a concise summary for a decision-maker.

```text
Write an executive summary of the following material.

Source material: {source_material}

Audience: {audience}

Decision or action they need to take: {decision_or_action}

The summary must:
1. Open with the single most important thing the reader needs to know
2. State the recommendation or conclusion directly — do not bury it
3. Give the two or three supporting reasons that matter most to this audience
4. Name the key risk or caveat they need to be aware of
5. Close with the specific action requested, if any

Constraints:
- Maximum 250 words
- No jargon the audience would not recognise
- Every sentence must earn its place — cut anything that does not change what the reader would decide or do
```
