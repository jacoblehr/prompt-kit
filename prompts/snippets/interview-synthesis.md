# Interview Synthesis

Use when you have conducted multiple qualitative interviews or conversations and need to extract patterns, not just quotes.

```text
Synthesise the following interview data.

Interviews or responses: {interview_data}

Research question or goal: {research_question}

Analyse:
1. Recurring themes — what comes up across multiple respondents, unprompted?
2. Divergent views — where do respondents disagree, and what might explain the split?
3. Surprising findings — what was said that you did not expect, or that contradicts assumptions?
4. Absence signals — what was conspicuously not mentioned by most respondents?
5. Emotional intensity — which topics generated the strongest reactions, positive or negative?
6. Quotable anchors — two or three direct quotes that best capture the overall picture

Then produce:
- A three-sentence synthesis of what the interviews collectively reveal
- The one thing that most challenges your prior assumptions
- The most important follow-up question this data raises
```
