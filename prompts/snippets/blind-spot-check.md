# Blind Spot Check

Use when you want to surface what you are probably not thinking about.

```text
Surface the blind spots I am probably missing here.

Look for:
- perspectives of stakeholders I may be underweighting
- common failure categories that apply to this type of problem
- hidden incentives or dynamics I may be ignoring
- the question I should be asking but am not
- evidence I might be filtering out because it is inconvenient

Return:
- blind spots identified
- most consequential blind spot
- the question I should be asking but am not
- what a skeptical outside observer would probably say

Context:
{paste context}
```
