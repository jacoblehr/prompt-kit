# Stakeholder Map

Use when buy-in, resistance, or coordination will shape the outcome.

```text
Map the stakeholders in this situation.

Return for each stakeholder:
- stakeholder
- goals
- likely concerns
- influence level
- current support level
- what they need to say yes
- best communication move

Then return:
- biggest alignment risk
- the single most important conversation to have next

Decision, project, or goal:
{paste the outcome you are trying to achieve}

Situation:
{paste situation}
```
