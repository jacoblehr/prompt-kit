# Meeting Prep

Use when you need to prepare for a conversation efficiently.

```text
Prepare me for this meeting.

Return:
- likely goals
- what I need to know
- smart questions to ask
- likely risks or tensions
- best outcome
- minimum acceptable outcome

My desired outcome:
{paste what I want from the meeting}

Context:
{paste meeting context}
```
