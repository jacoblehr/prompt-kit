# Trend Analysis

Use when scanning an environment for emerging shifts that could affect a strategy, market, or plan.

```text
Analyse the trends relevant to this domain or situation.

For each significant trend:
- name it and describe it in one sentence
- assess its trajectory: accelerating, stable, or reversing
- note the time horizon over which it will likely have material impact
- describe the direct first-order effects and the less-obvious second-order effects
- identify which current assumptions or strategies it challenges
- suggest the adaptive move it implies

Then:
- highlight the two or three trends most likely to compound or intersect
- identify any counter-trends that could slow or reverse the main pattern
- name the one development in this environment that most people are underweighting

Domain or situation:
{paste sector, challenge, or strategic context}
```
