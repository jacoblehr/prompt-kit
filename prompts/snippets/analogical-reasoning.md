# Analogical Reasoning

Use when a problem is stuck and a solution from a structurally similar domain might transfer.

```text
Find analogous problems from other domains and extract transferable solutions.

Requirements:
- identify the structural pattern or constraint at the core of this problem
- find three domains or fields that have solved structurally similar problems
- for each: describe the analogous problem, the solution used, and what specifically transfers
- identify the key differences that might limit transfer
- propose one concrete adaptation based on the best analogy

Problem:
{paste problem or challenge}

What makes it hard:
{paste the key constraint or tension}
```
