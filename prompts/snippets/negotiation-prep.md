# Negotiation Prep

Use when preparing for a negotiation, difficult conversation, or situation where conflicting interests need to be reconciled.

```text
Prepare a negotiation analysis for this situation.

Cover:
- our primary interests (what we actually need, beneath stated positions)
- their likely primary interests (infer from available context)
- our BATNA: best alternative if no agreement is reached
- their likely BATNA
- the zone of possible agreement (ZOPA): the range where both sides could accept a deal
- potential trades or package deals that create value for both sides
- the concession to give last: what seems costly to us but matters most to them
- the most likely objection they will raise, and how to address it honestly

Then recommend an opening position strategy: what to ask for first and how to set
a credible anchor.

Situation:
{paste negotiation context and background}

Our stated position vs. our actual interests:
{paste if you can distinguish them}
```
