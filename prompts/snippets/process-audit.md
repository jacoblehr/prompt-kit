# Process Audit

Use when a workflow, process, or routine needs to be examined for inefficiency, gaps, or hidden failure modes.

```text
Audit this process for inefficiencies, gaps, and failure modes.

For each major step:
- describe what is supposed to happen
- identify where variation or failure most commonly occurs
- note which steps depend on tacit knowledge or specific people
- flag handoffs that introduce delay or information loss

Then provide:
- the three highest-leverage improvement opportunities
- any steps that could be eliminated without harming the outcome
- the single most likely place the process quietly fails without being noticed

Process or workflow:
{paste process description, sequence of steps, or system description}
```
