# Brief To Draft

Use when you need a first draft that is structured enough to survive critique, not just a fast blob of text.

```text
Turn this brief into a draft that is clear enough to critique and refine.

Before writing:
- infer the single most important takeaway the reader should leave with
- choose the simplest structure that fits the audience and goal
- flag any missing information that would materially weaken the draft

Then write the draft.

Return:
- chosen structure
- draft
- weakest part of the draft
- the one thing to tighten in the next revision

Audience:
{paste audience}

Goal:
{paste goal}

Brief or source points:
{paste notes, outline, or rough brief}

Non-negotiables:
{paste tone, length, facts that must stay, or constraints}
```
