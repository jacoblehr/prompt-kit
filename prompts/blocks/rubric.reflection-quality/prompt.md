# Prompt

Evaluate this reflection or retrospective against the quality checklist.

For each criterion below, answer yes, partial, or no — and state the evidence in one sentence:

- Did this go beyond recap?
- Is there at least one reusable lesson?
- Is there at least one concrete change to make?
- Did it identify what actually caused the outcome?
- Did it avoid blame theater?
- Will this make a future decision or workflow better?

Then give:
- overall verdict: complete / needs more depth
- the single highest-leverage addition if more depth is needed
