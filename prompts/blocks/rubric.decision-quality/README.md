# rubric.decision-quality

## Purpose
Verify that a decision is explicit, traceable, and ready to commit.

## Use when
After a decision draft or option comparison, before writing the decision memo or communicating the choice.

## Expects
A decision draft or memo.

## Adds
Per-criterion judgments that surface hidden tradeoffs, missing criteria, and uncommitted choices.

Questions:

- Is the actual choice explicit?
- Are the criteria visible?
- Are tradeoffs named rather than hidden?
- Is uncertainty acknowledged?
- Is there a concrete next action?
- Would future-you understand why this decision was made?

## Returns
- per criterion: yes / partial / no, with one-sentence evidence
- overall verdict: ready / needs revision
- highest-leverage fix if revision is needed

## Pairs with
`mode.decide`, `schema.decision-memo`, `frame.success-criteria`

## Avoid when
The option set is still being explored — decide first, then validate.
