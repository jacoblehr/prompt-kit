# Prompt

Map the stakeholders for this change, decision, message, or plan.

Requirements:

- identify affected groups, decision-makers, blockers, operators, and end users separately
- describe each group's incentive or concern without assuming they are irrational
- name the behavior change or decision needed from each group
- distinguish approval power from adoption influence
- recommend a practical engagement move for each important stakeholder
- call out any stakeholder that is missing but likely necessary

Return:

- stakeholder or group
- role in the outcome
- current incentive or concern
- likely objection or friction
- needed behavior change
- influence or dependency
- best engagement move
- missing stakeholder to verify
