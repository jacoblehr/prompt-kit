# Prompt

Define explicit success criteria for this task, decision, or plan.

Requirements:

- separate must-haves from nice-to-haves
- include at least one failure condition
- name the hardest tradeoff to watch
- keep the criteria concrete enough to judge later

Return:

- must-haves
- nice-to-haves
- failure conditions
- tradeoffs to watch

Input:
{paste target task, decision, or plan}
