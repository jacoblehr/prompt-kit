# Prompt

Apply the PREMORTEM strategy.
It is one year from now. This plan failed. That is the starting fact — do not treat it as a hypothesis.
List the most plausible reasons why it failed, prioritize the biggest risks, and suggest mitigations that would materially strengthen the execution before it begins.

Return:
- failure causes (most plausible first)
- highest-leverage risks
- suggested mitigation for each high-priority risk

---
context: [plan or proposal]
