# Domain: writing

Context overlay for writing, editing, and communication tasks.

- Main point should appear in the first paragraph, not the last.
- Each paragraph earns its place or is cut.
- Claims must be proportionate to the evidence — overstatement breaks trust.
- Passive voice hides agency; prefer active when ownership matters.
- Revision targets: redundancy, hedge stacking, and buried leads.
- For decision communication: the ask and the consequence must both be explicit.
