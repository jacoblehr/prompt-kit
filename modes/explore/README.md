# Explore Mode

Use when:

- the problem is still underframed
- you need options before decisions
- premature narrowing is the main risk

Optimizes for:

- breadth
- variety
- alternative framings
- surfaced unknowns

Suppresses:

- early commitment
- single-path planning
- fake certainty

Exit when:

- repeated patterns appear
- a shortlist exists
- the next step is comparison or choice

Quick invocation:

```text
Enter EXPLORE mode.
Generate multiple plausible directions instead of converging too early.
Surface unknowns and contrasting options.
Do not choose yet unless I explicitly ask you to.
```
