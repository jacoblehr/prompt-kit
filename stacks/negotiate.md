# Stack: negotiate

Prepare and execute a negotiation with explicit interests, BATNA, and concession strategy.

Blocks:
1. `mode.explore`
2. `frame.task`
3. `strategy.steelman`
4. `mode.decide`
5. `schema.plan-next-actions`

Expected output: Preparation brief with counterpart interests mapped, BATNA stated, and opening position justified.

## Composition notes

`mode.explore` maps the counterpart's perspective before entering the conversation. `frame.task` defines the specific deal being negotiated. `strategy.steelman` models the counterpart's strongest position so concessions are prepared. `mode.decide` closes on an opening strategy. `schema.plan-next-actions` sequences the approach and fallback.

**Minimum blocks:** `frame.task` + `strategy.steelman`
