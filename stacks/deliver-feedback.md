# Stack: deliver-feedback

Give honest, actionable feedback without diluting the signal.

Blocks:
1. `mode.critique`
2. `guardrail.disconfirming-evidence`
3. `rubric.writing-quality`

Expected output: Prioritized findings with specific revision advice and no softening filler.

## Composition notes

`mode.critique` establishes a direct-signal lens over a diplomatic one. `guardrail.disconfirming-evidence` forces the strongest reading of the work before flagging defects. `rubric.writing-quality` shapes the feedback to be specific and actionable rather than impressionistic.

**Minimum blocks:** `mode.critique` + `rubric.writing-quality`
